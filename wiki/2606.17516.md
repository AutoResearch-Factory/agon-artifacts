## 精华提炼

### 管线/方法

FoundCause (internal codename "CDT" = Causal Discovery Transformer, visible throughout the LaTeX source though the public-facing name is FoundCause) is a fully amortized, feed-forward causal discovery model. The pipeline:

1. **Training-data generation** (entirely synthetic, no real data ever touches training): a pool of CPU workers continuously regenerates ~12,000 fresh tasks per epoch (10,000 multivariate + 2,000 bivariate). Half the tasks come from DoWhy's SCM generator (`sec:dowhy-path`), half from a custom generator with 5 graph topologies (Erdős–Rényi via sequential attachment, scale-free, small-world, stochastic block model, geometric/star) and 18+ nonlinear mechanism families (GP-RFF, tanh/sigmoid MLPs, Michaelis-Menten, Hill, piecewise-linear, polynomial, threshold-binary, categorical, etc.).
2. **Input**: a data matrix X ∈ R^{N×D} (D ∈ [2,50], N ∈ [100,600] at training) plus an optional missingness mask M. Each cell becomes a 2-channel vector [normalized-value·mask, mask], linearly projected to d_h=768. No positional embeddings on the variable axis — variables are identified purely by their statistical content, which is what makes the model variable-count-agnostic.
3. **Axis-factorized encoder**: 16 alternating attention blocks (8 variable-attention + 8 sample-attention), RMSNorm + SwiGLU, FlashAttention-compatible SDPA. Variable-attention blocks additionally receive (a) K_g=12 learnable global context tokens and (b) a stat-conditioned attention bias derived from 45 raw-data pairwise statistics.
4. **PMA pooling** (Set Transformer-style, Lee et al. 2019) over the sample axis collapses N samples per variable into one embedding z_i ∈ R^{768}, gated-blended with max-pooling (gate initialized near 0 so training starts equivalent to max-pool and gradually learns to use PMA).
5. **Factored edge decoder**: existence head (symmetric features: z_i+z_j, z_i⊙z_j, projected symmetric stats, detached confounding score) and direction head (antisymmetric features: z_i−z_j, gated antisymmetric stats) are computed separately, then fused. A parent/child role-score term (bilinear, low weight) adds an explicit asymmetric inductive bias.
6. **Triangular edge refinement**: a D×D×192 pair-feature tensor is refined for 3 rounds using AlphaFold2-Evoformer-inspired triangle updates (outgoing/chain, incoming/fork, collider) plus row-wise self-attention — explicitly targets higher-order motifs (chains, forks, colliders / V-structures) that pairwise-independent decoders cannot represent.
7. **Confounder module**: 8 learnable confounder tokens cross-attend to variable embeddings; per-(variable,token) loadings S_ik combine via noisy-OR into a symmetric confounding matrix Ĉ. Ĉ feeds back into the existence head via `.detach()` so edge-prediction gradients cannot suppress confounder learning.
8. **Output**: directed edge probabilities D̂ ∈ [0,1]^{D×D} and confounding probabilities Ĉ ∈ [0,1]^{D×D}, produced in <2 seconds per dataset at inference, versus classical methods that may take hours.
9. **Inference-time post-processing** (fixed across all evaluations, no per-dataset tuning): K=10 stochastic runs (bootstrap resampling + random variable permutation, results averaged back to original order), temperature scaling (T=0.65), two-component GMM adaptive thresholding, self-consistency pruning (keep edges appearing in ≥50% of runs), greedy DAG post-processing for acyclicity.

### 关键技术细节

- **Scale**: ~139M parameters. d_h=768, 8 attention heads, FFN inner dim 2048 (SwiGLU, 2/3×4d_h), pair dim d_pair=192, 3 triangular refinement rounds, K_c=8 confounder tokens, K_g=12 global tokens, K_q=8 PMA query tokens.
- **45 pairwise statistics** feed the attention bias, existence/direction heads, and confounder module: 9 symmetric (Pearson, Spearman, partial correlation via Ledoit-Wolf shrinkage, skewness/kurtosis products, collider evidence, partial-correlation gap, quadratic cross-dependence), 20 antisymmetric (12 differenced-direction pairs — Nadaraya-Watson residual variance, HSIC-RFF, cross-moments, Chatterjee's ξ, IGCI-variance, precision-regression coefficients — plus 8 directly antisymmetric like skewness/kurtosis difference), 4 V-structure, 4 "reliability metadata" (condition number, D/N ratio, unique-value ratio, sample-count) that are *never* dropped during training so the model can learn when other stats are unreliable.
- **Feature dropout as the only regularizer**: no dropout anywhere in the network; antisymmetric/V-structure stats get inverted dropout (p=0.2) during training, forcing reliance on both learned and classical signals; continuous data regeneration is the primary regularizer.
- **Anti-shortcut augmentation**: 100% of batches get variable permutation (removing the Reisach et al. 2023 variance-ordering shortcut); 5% subpopulation-mixture tasks (two SCMs' samples pooled, DAG = union) for heterogeneous-population robustness; 12% "correlation fog" (near-deterministic dense linear SCMs, noise std ∈[0.001,0.05]) forces distinguishing direct edges from strong indirect correlation; 8% intervention-mixture tasks.
- **10-term composite loss**: primary adaptive-recall-weighted BCE (L_dir) + 9 auxiliary terms — asymmetric-direction penalty (suppresses reverse-edge confidence past a dead-zone), calibration penalty (caps |ℓ_ij − ℓ_ji| ≤ 4.0; authors state unconstrained logit gaps regressed Tübingen accuracy from 60.9%→54.6%, i.e. overconfidence directly hurt real-world transfer), all-edge direction loss, skeleton loss, confounding loss (capped weight to avoid gradient cascade — authors report λ_conf=1.0 caused 15× gradient amplification vs 1.0× at λ_conf=0.2), false-positive penalty (adaptive via precision EMA), density penalty, PMA-diversity regularizer, gate-bounds regularizer.
- **Optimizer**: Schedule-Free AdamW (Defazio et al. 2024), lr 4e-4 cosine-decayed to 1e-5 over 300 epochs then flat, 1000 epochs total, batch 24 (3×8 A100 GPUs, bfloat16 mixed precision with float32 for numerically sensitive ops).
- **Training data is 100% synthetic** — none of the 15 real-world benchmarks or Tübingen pairs are ever seen during training; the confounding ground truth C* is defined precisely as "observed i,j share a hidden common ancestor reachable only through hidden nodes" under a marginalized-observed DoWhy process.

### 核心结果

| Benchmark | FoundCause | Best classical | Best amortized baseline |
|---|---|---|---|
| 15 real-world datasets, AUROC | **0.756** | GRaSP 0.747 | SEA 0.744 |
| 15 real-world datasets, F1 | **0.535** | GRaSP 0.488 | SEA 0.512 |
| 15 real-world datasets, SHD/d | **0.980** | GRaSP 1.454 | SEA 1.052 |
| 15 real-world datasets, Skel-F1 | 0.663 | GRaSP **0.676** | SEA 0.667 |
| Coverage | 15/15 | PC/FCI: 13/15 (timeout on dense petshop) | 15/15 |
| Tübingen pairs (102, unweighted acc.) | 63.7% | ICA-LiNGAM 51.0% | SEA 57.8% (best amortized) — but bivariate-specialist SLOPPY 72.5%, HECI 70.4% both beat FoundCause |

- Dimension generalization (zero-shot, trained on D∈[2,50]): AUROC degrades gracefully to 0.712 at D=100 (2× training ceiling), but F1 falls faster (0.554→0.278) because the fixed decision threshold — calibrated in-distribution — becomes miscalibrated as candidate-pair count grows as D(D−1); authors attribute this explicitly to threshold miscalibration rather than representation collapse, since relative edge ranking (AUROC) survives much better than the thresholded F1. Confirmed on 4 real datasets D∈[56,100] (Hailfinder, HEPAR II, WIN95PT, DREAM4-100), AUROC 0.661–0.789 with no retraining.
- Only method (of 15 compared) that simultaneously covers all 15 datasets, reports latent confounding, handles missing data natively, and is variable-count agnostic (Table `tab:fm-comparison`).

### 明确局限

- **Reported by the authors**: underperforms specialized bivariate direction methods (SLOPPY, HECI) on Tübingen pairs by ~7–9 points; skeleton-F1 (undirected adjacency) is slightly below GRaSP; F1 degrades faster than AUROC under dimension extrapolation past D=50, and the paper is candid that this is a threshold-calibration artifact rather than a representational one.
- **No time/lag structure**: the model consumes X ∈ R^{N×D} as N i.i.d. cross-sectional samples — there is no notion of temporal order, lag, or sequence within a dataset. This is a fundamentally different problem framing from time-series causal discovery (PCMCI+, LKIF, VAR-based methods): FoundCause cannot be dropped in wherever lagged conditional independence or Granger-style temporal precedence is the identifying signal, without an explicit lag-augmentation step that changes what "conditioning" means.
- **Confounding ground truth is itself simulator-defined**: C* is only as good as the DoWhy hide-and-reveal procedure (marginalize a subset of nodes with ≥2 children); this is a common limitation across the amortized-CD literature but worth flagging since the paper's central novelty claim (first amortized method to explicitly model latent confounding) rests on training against this synthetic ground truth, never against real confounding.
- **Data-integrity concern found only by reading the raw .tex source (not visible in the rendered PDF alone)**: `missing_data_ablation.tex` opens with an un-rendered LaTeX comment: *"NOTE: The numbers below are PREDICTIONS based on architectural reasoning and the clean-data baseline. Replace with measured values from a controlled MAR run before submission."* This header precedes **both** the Test-Time Ablation Study section and the Robustness to Missing Data (10%/30% MAR) section.
  - The **test-time ablation table** (`tab:ablation-results-expected`) does partially self-disclose in its *visible* caption: "ablation rows are architectural predictions" — but the surrounding prose (Section "Ablations", "Results from Ablations") reads throughout as though six ablations were actually executed ("we perform test-time ablations...", "Table X indicates the largest gains arise from...").
  - The **missing-data MAR tables** (`tab:missing-10`, `tab:missing-30`) carry **no such caveat anywhere in their visible captions or surrounding prose** — a reader of the compiled PDF has no way to know these numbers might be estimates rather than measurements; the only signal is the un-rendered source comment.
  - This means two of the paper's supporting robustness claims ("graceful degradation under missingness, retains 92% of clean F1 at 30% MAR" and the entire component-ablation attribution narrative) may rest on numbers the authors themselves flagged internally as placeholders never replaced before arXiv submission. The headline 15-dataset comparison table and the Tübingen/dimension-generalization results carry no such caveat and appear to be genuine measurements.

### 跟我们的关系

**撞车评估**: 不构成 novelty 撞车。FoundCause 解决的是横截面 (i.i.d. 样本) 表格数据的静态因果发现 + 潜在混淆恢复，训练/推理全程没有时间轴、没有 lag、没有 tau_max 概念；0710-causal-scs 课题的核心是"因果*估计*在滑动时间窗口下的不稳定性能否作为强对流预警信号"，方法基础是显式处理时序滞后条件独立的 PCMCI+/LKIF。两者问题设定完全正交，FoundCause 的三个"首创"卖点 (统计特征偏置注意力、三角精化、显式混淆 token) 都是静态图恢复精度的改进，不触及我们 v3 idea 里 C1/C1b/C4 等 claim 涉及的"条件化 vs 边际相关""趋近分岔 vs 已变化"等时序脆弱性问题。

**可能有用的角度**（供 refine 时参考，非当前必须采纳）:

1. **速度换多 seed/bootstrap**：v3 review 明确把"多 seed 复现 + 两阶段 (calibration+evaluation) bootstrap"列为比 LKIF 复现更优先、更便宜的下一步，但当前瓶颈是 PCMCI+ 每次重跑的 CPU 成本。FoundCause 单次前向 <2 秒的推理速度，理论上如果能适配进滑窗协议，可以把"重跑一次因果估计"的成本从"一次 PCMCI+ 搜索"降到"一次前向传播"，让多 seed/两阶段 bootstrap 几乎免费——这正是回答 review 中"0.705/0.482 这两个点估计本身有多稳"的先决问题。
2. **直接替换的方法论代价**：FoundCause 假设样本 i.i.d.、无自相关，把它套进滑动窗口 (窗口内本质是强自相关的时间序列) 会违背其训练分布假设，且丢失 PCMCI+ 的 tau_max 滞后条件独立结构——"条件化"的含义会从"是否条件化于同期第三变量"变成"是否条件化于窗口内其他变量的横截面关系"，改变了实验在测什么。若要用，必须显式做 lag-augmentation (把 X_{t-1...t-τ} 当额外变量喂入)，这本身该是 Experiments 里一个需要讨论的选择，而非可隐藏的实现细节。
3. **混淆物模块的潜在延伸**：FoundCause 的 noisy-OR 混淆矩阵 Ĉ 直接对应我们 topic 的动机之一 (confounder 假设违背)。Ĉ 在滑窗间的波动理论上可以成为 (D,J) 双通道之外的第三个候选不稳定性信号，但这是一个全新的、未经验证的组件，建议只作为 factorial 设计定案后的探索性扩展，不要在当前 review 要求的"隔离条件化这一单一变量"的受控实验完成前引入。
4. **估计器泛化检验的候选**：如果要检验"causal conditioning penalty 是否跨估计器一般"，FoundCause 可以作为除 PCMCI+、LKIF 之外的第三个方法论截然不同 (amortized/学习式 vs 约束式/信息流) 的估计器，但由于它不处理时序，应放在 LKIF 复现 (Experiments #1) 之后、且需明确说明其经过 lag-augmentation 改造，不能直接替代 LKIF 复现。

**更值得关注的相邻文献**（本文引用列表中发现，比 FoundCause 本身更贴近我们的问题）: FoundCause 自己的 related work 中引用了 **"Embracing the black box: Heading towards foundation models for causal discovery from time series data" (arXiv:2402.09305)** —— 这篇才是真正的"时间序列因果发现基础模型"，问题设定比 FoundCause 更接近我们的滑窗协议，值得下一轮搜索优先精读，判断它是否已经把"用基础模型代替 PCMCI+ 做滑窗因果估计"这条路走过。

**引用建议**: 可作为"amortized/foundation-model 式因果发现"这一相关但正交子领域的代表作引用，尤其是在预期审稿人问题"为什么不直接用现成的因果发现基础模型做滑窗估计"时，可以用 FoundCause 的 i.i.d. 假设与我们时序滞后设定的不兼容性作为方法选择的正面论证依据。
